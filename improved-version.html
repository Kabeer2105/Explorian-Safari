Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before booking your adventure

Do I need a visa to visit Tanzania? ‚ñº
Most nationalities can obtain a visa on arrival at major entry points for $50-100 USD. E-visas are also available online. Check with your local embassy for specific requirements based on your nationality.
When is the best time to visit for safaris? ‚ñº
Tanzania offers excellent safari opportunities year-round. The dry season (June-October) is best for wildlife viewing. The Great Migration is best seen January-March (calving) and July-September (river crossings). Green season (November-May) offers lush landscapes and fewer tourists.
What fitness level is required for Kilimanjaro? ‚ñº
You don't need to be an athlete, but good fitness helps. We recommend regular cardio exercise for 3-4 months before your climb. Mental determination is equally important. Choose longer routes (7-9 days) for better acclimatization and higher success rates.
What's included in your safari packages? ‚ñº
Our packages typically include: park fees, professional guide, 4x4 safari vehicle, accommodation, meals as specified, drinking water, and transfers. Flights, visas, travel insurance, tips, and personal expenses are not included. We provide detailed inclusions for each package.
Are your tours family-friendly? ‚ñº
Absolutely! We offer family-friendly safari options with child discounts. Some parks like Arusha NP and Tarangire are excellent for children. We can arrange private vehicles, shorter game drives, and family accommodations. Kilimanjaro climbs have minimum age restrictions (typically 10 years).
How do I make a booking and payment? ‚ñº
Contact us via email, WhatsApp, or the contact form. We'll send a detailed itinerary and quote. A deposit (typically 30-50%) secures your booking, with balance due before arrival. We accept bank transfers, credit cards (via secure payment gateway), and online payments.
